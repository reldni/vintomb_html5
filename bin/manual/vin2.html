Playing the Game (Instructions):

 

On certain scenes, you can move Vinnie around. Vinnie can pick up objects he finds.

To Move Vinnie

  1. Click on Vinnie with the mouse

  2. Your mouse pointer should turn into Vinnie.

 

To collect an object

  1. Make sure your mouse pointer looks like Vinnie.

  2. Click on the object to try and pick it up.

 

To complete some scenes you will need to click on a hand pointing to the exit.

 

On some scenes, you must talk to creatures. Use the horizontal scrollbar at the bottom of the scene's window to advance the conversation. Normally, you cannot pickup objects or move Vinnie until you have completed the dialogue.

 

To use an object you have collected

  1. Click on the desired object located in your toolbox window. It should load at the top of your screen.

  2. Move your mouse on to the scene's window. Normally the mouse pointer will have changed into the object.

  3. Click and move the the object around the scene.

The toolbox window may not always be available.

 

To end the game at anytime click on the top right corner of the window.

 

As this is the first game in the trilogy, we have decided to make it relatively easy. The second chapter of Vinnie's Tomb will be more challenging. The second chapter should be available late in the summer of 1997.

 

Thank you for playing. We hope you enjoy the game.
